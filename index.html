Compliance IS Our Business

When Compliance Gets Too Difficult, Let Us Help You Simplify and Systematize Your Operations.

Welcome to RightShift Services - Helping you comply.

We are a business consultancy firm specializing in anti-money laundering and counter financing of terrorism (AML/CFT) compliance support services primarily for Money Services Businesses (MSBs) and Trust Companies.

Services

Policy and Procedure Manuals Preparation

 Assistance in Preparation of Policy and Procedure Manuals, Branch Supervisory Framework, Business Continuity Plans.

AML/CFT Advisory Services

 Advise the Client on all regulatory compliances not restricted to KYC and AML/CFT matters stipulated by the Central Bank and/or LFSA relating to the Client’s Money Services Business or Trust Company.

Business Plans and Feasibility Studies

 Undertake Branch Feasibility Studies, Company Business Plans including Cash Flow Projections and related Professional Services.

Advisory Services for Better Compliance and Efficiency

 Advise Clients on the systems and controls to be effected for better compliances to the regulations with a view to improving operational efficiency and effectiveness.

Specific Consultancy on Rectification Measures

 Advise the Client on compliance related matters and rectification of deviations.

Compliance Audit

 Conduct compliance audits as and when required and submitting the Audit report to the management/Board to rectify deviations.

What sets us apart?

• • Two of our key directors are ex-Central Bank senior executives with the experience and knowledge of understanding and interpreting the expectations of the regulator.

• • Our experience, knowledge and certification in AML/CFT by the International Compliance Association.

• • Advisory services in areas surrounding regulatory compliance, systems and controls, policies.

• • A scalable business model to best suit your needs including personalized 1-1 and private training sessions as well as consultancy services that can be organized around the country.

• • We provide services to a very niche group of companies within the financial services industry.


On-site Expertise and Knowledge

• One of the directors previously headed the Special Investigations Unit in the Central Bank where he was entrusted to conduct on-site investigation of offences.

Knowledge and Experience in Systemizing Operations and Implementing Controls

• The other director held a senior level role within the IT services industry line with over 20 years of experience and a solid background in systems, methods and controls.
